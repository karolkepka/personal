Bank of Lithuania
Central bank · governed cloud & fair hyperscaler comparison
Sponsors Bank of Lithuania

Essence: democratize access
under strong governance — then choose cloud with evidence

The bank aimed for broader, accountable use of cloud for data and analytics: easier consumption for teams, with controls and oversight appropriate to a central bank. A wider assessment was commissioned to inform hyperscaler choice. I led the requirements track: gathering input from multiple internal stakeholder groups, consolidating it, and mapping needs into architectural, functional, and non-functional criteria evaluated evenly against AWS and Microsoft Azure. This phase was co-funded by AWS and Microsoft. This slide states only the public-safe shape of the work.
